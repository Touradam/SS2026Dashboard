# Quick Start Guide

## Get Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Open the Dashboard
1. Navigate to the project folder
2. Double-click `index.html`
3. The dashboard will open in your default browser

**Alternative**: Right-click `index.html` → Open with → Choose your browser

### Step 2: Explore the Dashboard

The dashboard has 8 main sections:

1. **📊 Overview** - See progress at a glance
   - Current week indicator
   - Days remaining
   - Overall completion percentage

2. **📅 Timeline** - Browse all 26 weeks
   - Color-coded by phase
   - Click any week for details
   - Current week is highlighted

3. **📆 Monthly** - View month-by-month
   - Tab between July-December
   - Expand weeks for full details
   - See monthly objectives

4. **🤝 Relationships** - Key contacts
   - PnP Core Network (4 contacts)
   - Manufacturing Partners (2 partners)
   - Pilot Champions (3 customers)
   - Search to find specific people

5. **✅ Readiness** - Track your progress
   - 14 critical checklist items
   - Check off items as you complete them
   - See real-time progress updates
   - Progress saves automatically

6. **🎯 Milestones** - Q3 & Q4 goals
   - Major quarterly objectives
   - Track completion criteria
   - Visual progress indicators

7. **📍 Key Dates** - Important events
   - 12 critical dates
   - Countdown to next event
   - Filter by type

8. **📖 Glossary** - Quick reference
   - 14 acronyms explained
   - Key contact information
   - Search functionality

### Step 3: Track Your Progress

**To mark items as complete:**
1. Scroll to the Readiness section
2. Check the boxes for completed items
3. Watch your progress update automatically
4. Your progress is saved in your browser

**To track milestones:**
1. Go to the Milestones section
2. Check off completed criteria
3. See your Q3 and Q4 progress

**To export your progress:**
1. Scroll to the bottom of the page
2. Click "Export Progress"
3. A JSON file will download with your data

## Tips & Tricks

### Navigation
- Use the top menu to jump between sections
- On mobile, tap the menu icon (☰) to see all options
- All links scroll smoothly to the right section

### Search Features
- **Relationships**: Type a name, role, or organization
- **Glossary**: Search for any acronym or term
- Both update results as you type

### Filters
- **Key Dates**: Click filter buttons to show specific event types
- "All" shows everything
- Choose Program, Travel, Manufacturing, Deployment, Milestone, or Holiday

### Progress Tracking
- ✅ Your progress is saved automatically in your browser
- 🔄 You can use the dashboard on multiple devices, but progress won't sync between them
- 💾 Export your progress regularly to keep backups
- 🗑️ Click "Reset All" to start over (can't be undone!)

### Week Details
- Click any week card in the Timeline section
- A popup shows complete details
- Press ESC or click outside to close

## Keyboard Shortcuts

## Troubleshooting

### Dashboard won't open
- ✅ Make sure you have all files in the same folder
- ✅ Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- ✅ Check that `data.js`, `app.js`, and `styles.css` are present

### Progress not saving
- ✅ Make sure cookies/storage is enabled in your browser
- ✅ Don't use private/incognito mode (storage is cleared when closed)
- ✅ Check browser console (F12) for errors

### Sections not displaying
- ✅ Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- ✅ Clear your browser cache
- ✅ Make sure JavaScript is enabled

### Layout looks broken
- ✅ Try zooming to 100% (Ctrl+0 or Cmd+0)
- ✅ Update your browser to the latest version
- ✅ Check window width (dashboard needs at least 320px)
