## 🎉 What Was Built

A comprehensive, fully-functional project management dashboard to track the 6-month Solar Sense Pro China market entry execution plan (July - December 2026).

### Core Features Implemented

#### 1. Dashboard Overview ✅
- Hero section with project timeline and branding
- 4 interactive progress metric cards:
  - Overall completion percentage
  - Current week indicator (auto-calculated)
  - Days remaining countdown
  - Checklist progress tracker
- Quick stats showing 26 weeks, 6 phases, 1,070 total hours

#### 2. Interactive Timeline ✅
- 26 week cards with phase-based color coding
- Current week auto-highlighting based on today's date
- Click any week to see full details in modal
- Priority badges (Critical, High, Medium)
- Hour estimates for Adama and Jordan
- All weeks extracted from source document

#### 3. Monthly Objectives Dashboard ✅
- Tab navigation for all 6 months (July - December)
- Each month shows:
  - Primary focus statement
  - Pilot acquisition goals
  - Manufacturing goals
  - Critical deliverables
- Accordion-style week details
- Complete technical, relationship, and Jordan tracks

#### 4. Relationship Matrix ✅
- 9 key contacts organized in 3 categories:
  - PnP Core Network (4 contacts)
  - Manufacturing Partners (2 partners)
  - Pilot Champions (3 customers)
- Each contact card shows:
  - Role and name
  - Organization
  - Why it matters
  - Sourcing path
  - Next action
  - First ask
  - Status badge with color coding
- Real-time search functionality

#### 5. Launch Readiness Checklist ✅
- 14 critical checklist items across 3 categories:
  - Active Pilot & Field Operations (4 items)
  - Validated Manufacturing & Supply Chain (7 items)
  - Strategic Network & Autonomy (3 items)
- Interactive checkboxes with localStorage persistence
- Real-time progress calculation
- Category completion tracking
- Status indicators (Not Started, In Progress, Near Completion, Complete)
- All verification criteria and target dates included

#### 6. Quarterly Milestones ✅
- Q3 2026 milestone (Sept 30) with 5 criteria
- Q4 2026 milestone (Dec 31) with 6 criteria
- Interactive completion tracking
- Visual progress bars
- All success criteria from source document

#### 7. Key Dates Calendar ✅
- 12 critical dates from July 2026 to January 2027
- Countdown banner to next upcoming event
- Filter by type: Program, Travel, Manufacturing, Deployment, Milestone, Holiday
- All dates with locations and priority levels

#### 8. Glossary & Quick Reference ✅
- 14 acronyms with full definitions
- 3 key contacts (Aboli, Jordan, PnP China)
- Search functionality
- Complete contact information and responsibilities
